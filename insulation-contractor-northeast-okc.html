Insulation Challenges in NE OKC's Housing Stock

Northeast Oklahoma City's housing spans decades of construction, and insulation needs vary significantly depending on when and how each home was built:

  • Lincoln Park Area (1940s-1960s) — The neighborhoods surrounding Lincoln Park and the Oklahoma City Zoo contain some of NE OKC's oldest homes. Many of these modest frame homes were built with little or no insulation. Wall cavities are typically empty, attic insulation is thin or nonexistent, and decades of settling have created air leaks throughout. These homes respond dramatically to injection foam walls, blown-in attic insulation, and air sealing — often cutting energy bills by 30 to 50 percent.
  • NE 36th to NE 63rd Corridor — Mid-century homes in this area were built during the 1950s and 1960s when insulation practices were minimal. Fiberglass batts, if installed at all, have settled and compressed over 60 years. Many of these homes also have single-pane windows and poor weatherstripping, compounding the insulation deficiency. A comprehensive insulation and air sealing upgrade transforms both comfort and energy efficiency.
  • NE 63rd to NE 122nd Area — This zone features a mix of 1970s through 1990s homes with some newer construction. The older homes in this range have aging, code-minimum insulation that has lost effectiveness over the decades. Newer homes may have been built to code but still benefit from upgraded attic insulation and air sealing, particularly if they have bonus rooms, vaulted ceilings, or rooms that never seem to reach the right temperature.
  • Spencer, Jones & Rural NE OKC — The further northeast you go, the more rural the properties become. Homes in these areas range from older farmhouses to 1970s-1980s ranch homes to newer construction. Many older rural homes have significant insulation deficiencies, and the open terrain means they take the full brunt of Oklahoma's north winds in winter. Proper insulation is not a luxury here — it is essential for comfort and manageable utility bills.

Affordable Insulation for NE OKC Homes

As your neighbors, we understand the value of honest pricing and real results. Insulation upgrades in NE OKC deliver measurable savings on every utility bill.

The cost of insulating a NE OKC home depends on its size, age, and current condition.
