Insulation Options for New Homes in Mustang

New construction provides the best opportunity to install optimal insulation because all surfaces are accessible. Here is what we install in Mustang's new homes:

Open-Cell Spray Foam — The Comfort Upgrade

More Mustang homebuyers are requesting spray foam insulation than ever before. Open-cell spray foam applied to the roof deck creates a conditioned attic that eliminates the extreme temperature swings that stress HVAC systems. In Mustang, where summer highs regularly exceed 100 degrees and winter lows can drop below 10, a conditioned attic can reduce heating and cooling costs by 30-50% compared to a traditional vented attic with blown-in insulation on the floor. Open-cell spray foam costs $1.50-$2.50 per square foot and is the single most impactful insulation upgrade for new Mustang homes.

Closed-Cell Spray Foam — Premium Protection

Closed-cell spray foam delivers the highest R-value per inch (approximately R-6.5) and serves as both an insulation and a moisture barrier. In Mustang new construction, closed-cell foam is most commonly used in crawl spaces, rim joists, and bonus rooms over garages — areas where moisture resistance and maximum thermal performance are critical. At $2.50-$4.00 per square foot, it is the premium option, but its dual function as insulation and vapor retarder eliminates the need for separate moisture management materials.

Blown-In Fiberglass — Reliable and Affordable

Dense-pack blown-in fiberglass provides excellent cavity fill in wall framing, flowing around electrical wires and plumbing to eliminate the gaps common with hand-cut batts. For Mustang builders who need dependable thermal performance at a competitive price, blown-in fiberglass is an outstanding choice for walls. In attics, it can be installed to any depth to achieve code-required R-values. Many Mustang builders specify blown-in walls with a spray foam attic as their standard insulation package.

Fiberglass Batts — The Cost Leader

Traditional fiberglass batts offer the lowest per-square-foot cost and remain widely used in Mustang's new construction market. Bo's Insulation installs batts to Grade I standards, which means every batt is cut precisely to fit, with no gaps around outlets, pipes, or wiring, and no compression that reduces R-value. When batts are installed correctly, they provide reliable thermal performance that meets code at the most affordable price point. For entry-level and mid-range Mustang homes where keeping construction costs competitive is essential, quality batt installation is a smart choice.
