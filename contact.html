What to Expect: Our Free Estimate Process

Getting a quote from Bo's Insulation is easy, fast, and completely free. Here's how it works.

1

Give Us a Call

Call (405) 437-0146 during business hours (Mon-Fri, 8AM-5PM). Tell us about your home and what you're experiencing — high energy bills, drafts, uneven temperatures, or anything else. We'll answer your initial questions right on the phone.

2

Schedule Your Free Visit

We'll set up a convenient time to come to your home. We work around your schedule and always show up on time. No waiting around for a 4-hour service window.

3

On-Site Assessment

We'll inspect your attic, walls, crawl spaces, and other areas to understand exactly where your home is losing energy. We check insulation levels, look for air leaks, and identify problem areas — then explain everything we find in plain English.

4

Honest Quote — No Pressure

You'll get a clear, written estimate with no hidden fees. We'll explain what we recommend, why we recommend it, and what it will cost. If the work doesn't make sense for your home, we'll tell you. We never pressure you into a decision — take your time.

5

Quality Installation

If you decide to move forward, we'll schedule the work at a time that works for you. Most projects are completed in a single day. We clean up after ourselves and make sure you're 100% satisfied before we leave.

Estimate FAQ

Is the estimate really free?

Yes — 100% free with absolutely no obligation. We'll come to your home, assess your insulation needs, and give you a written quote. There's no charge whether you hire us or not. We believe an honest estimate is the best way to earn your trust and your business.

How long does the estimate visit take?

A typical on-site estimate takes about 30 to 45 minutes. We'll inspect your attic, check wall insulation levels, look for air leaks, and discuss your comfort concerns. You'll usually have your written quote the same day or within 24 hours for larger projects.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the project — you never pay until the work is done and you're satisfied with the results.
