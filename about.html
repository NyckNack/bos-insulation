Meet the Bohannan Brothers

Two hardworking Oklahomans on a mission to make OKC homes more comfortable — and give back to the community while doing it.

Our Story

Bo's Insulation started the way most good things do in Oklahoma — with family, hard work, and a handshake.

Keyton and Nicholas Bohannan grew up in Oklahoma City. They learned early that if you want something done right, you do it yourself — and if you give someone your word, you keep it. Those values weren't just lessons; they became the foundation of a business.

The brothers noticed something that frustrated them about the insulation industry: too many contractors cutting corners, overcharging homeowners, and pushing services people didn't need. Older homes in the OKC metro were leaking energy and money, and the people who lived in them deserved better.

So Keyton and Nicholas started Bo's Insulation with a simple promise: show up on time, do honest work at honest prices, and treat every home like it's their own. No slick sales tactics. No hidden fees. Just two brothers who know insulation inside and out, doing right by their neighbors.

More than eight years later, that approach has earned Bo's Insulation a perfect 5.0-star rating across every platform — Google, HomeAdvisor, Angi, Thumbtack, and the BBB. With 89+ five-star reviews from real OKC homeowners, the Bohannan brothers have built something rare: an insulation company that people actually trust and recommend to their friends.

Our Mission

We exist to make Oklahoma City homes more comfortable, energy-efficient, and affordable to live in — while treating every homeowner with the honesty they deserve.

Every project starts with a free, no-pressure estimate. We assess your home, explain exactly what it needs (and what it doesn't), and give you a fair price upfront. If insulation work doesn't make sense for your home, we'll tell you — even if it means we don't get the job.

We believe that doing the right thing is always good business. That means using quality materials, standing behind our work, and never cutting corners to save a few dollars. It means answering your questions honestly instead of steering you toward the most expensive option. And it means showing up when we say we will and finishing when we say we'll finish.

20% of Every Project Goes to Charity

When you hire Bo's Insulation, you're not just investing in your home. You're investing in your community.

This isn't a marketing gimmick or a seasonal promotion. Since the beginning, Bo's Insulation has donated 20% of the proceeds from every single project to local nonprofits serving the Oklahoma City community.

Why 20%? Because Keyton and Nicholas believe that a business should do more than just make money. They grew up in this community. Their neighbors, friends, and families live here. And they've seen firsthand how local nonprofits make a real difference — feeding families, supporting kids, and building a stronger Oklahoma City.

Every time you choose Bo's Insulation, a meaningful portion of that investment goes directly back into the community. It's built into our pricing from day one, so you're never paying a premium for it. It's simply how we do business — because we believe that building up the people around us is just as important as insulating their homes.

We partner with local nonprofits throughout the OKC metro area, focusing on organizations that have a direct, measurable impact on the families and communities we serve every day.

Why We Only Do Existing Homes

We don't do new construction. Not because we can't — because we choose not to.

Insulating an existing home is a completely different challenge than insulating new construction. Older homes have settled foundations, irregular wall cavities, decades of wear, hidden air leaks, and unique quirks that you only discover once you start looking. A 1960s ranch in Edmond has different needs than a 1980s two-story in Norman, and both are worlds apart from a 1940s bungalow in the Paseo.

By focusing exclusively on existing homes, we've spent eight-plus years mastering the specific challenges that Oklahoma City homeowners face. We know where older homes lose the most energy. We know which insulation types work best in different wall constructions. We know how to insulate a finished wall without tearing it open. And we know how Oklahoma's extreme weather — from 110-degree summers to ice storms in February — punishes homes that aren't properly insulated.

That focused expertise means better results for you. We're not dividing our attention between commercial jobs, new builds, and residential retrofits. Every project, every crew member, and every hour of experience we have is dedicated to making existing Oklahoma homes more comfortable and energy-efficient.

When you hire a specialist, you get specialist-level results.
